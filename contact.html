About Cincinnati Volleyball Club

Cincinnati Volleyball Club (CVC) is a passionate community of volleyball enthusiasts located in the heart of Ohio. Established to promote competitive and recreational volleyball, CVC offers a platform for players of all skill levels to connect, grow, and compete.

We proudly host the 17th Annual Volleyball Tournament and 5th Annual Golf Tournament in 2025, bringing together over 50 teams and approximately 600 participants from major Mid-Western cities including Columbus, Cleveland, Chicago, Indianapolis, Detroit, Nashville, Louisville, Lexington, Memphis, and Atlanta.

Our events provide excellent sponsorship opportunities for businesses looking to engage with the community and market to a targeted audience of hotel owners and sports enthusiasts.
