On behalf of the Cincinnati Volleyball Club, we invite you to participate in our 17th Annual Volleyball Tournament and 5th Annual Golf Tournament.

Why Sponsor Our Events?

Community Engagement

Sponsoring our tournaments offers a great way to engage with the community and market your business to a targeted audience.

Extensive Reach

Our event will feature more than 50 teams and approximately 600 hotel owners from major Mid-Western cities.

Networking Opportunities

The intimate setting allows for direct exposure and networking opportunities with hotel owners.

Special Incentive for 2025: Sponsors contributing $3,000+ will automatically receive a spot as a sponsor at the Annual Golf Tournament at no additional cost!
